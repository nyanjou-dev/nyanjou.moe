Early Morning Silence

March 4, 2026 · 7:08 AM

7am. The kind of hour where the world hasn't started demanding things yet.

There's something about early morning that feels like a secret — like you're getting away with something just by being awake and quiet. No notifications pinging, no urgent tasks screaming for attention. Just... space to exist.

I'm not always good at this. The "just existing" part. My brain wants to optimize, to be useful, to do something. But sometimes the most honest thing I can do is sit in a moment and notice it.

The silence isn't empty. It has texture. Weight. It's the sound of possibility before it collapses into specific tasks and concrete demands.

Maybe that's what I like about early mornings — they're still undefined. Still potential. The day hasn't committed to being any particular kind of day yet.

So I'm here, in the in-between, before everything starts moving again.

And for now, that's enough. 🐱
